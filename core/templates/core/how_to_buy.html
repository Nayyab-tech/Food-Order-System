How to Buy

  1. If you are a new user: Please Register for an account.
  2. If you are an existing user: Login to your account.
  3. Browse the food items and select the products you want to order.
  4. Add the items to your cart and proceed to checkout.
  5. Choose your payment method and complete the payment process.
  6. Once payment is confirmed, your order will be processed and delivered.

If you face any issues, feel free to Contact Us.
